Learn About Mental Health

Understanding mental health is the first step to taking care of yourself and others.

Key Topics in Mental Health

Some important areas to learn about:

  • Stress Management
  • Depression and Anxiety
  • Healthy Habits and Mindfulness
  • Supporting Others

Steps to improve mental health awareness:

  1. Learn about common mental health conditions
  2. Practice mindfulness and self-care
  3. Reach out for help when needed
  4. Share knowledge with friends and family

Common Mental Health Conditions

Condition Symptoms Suggested Actions
Anxiety Excessive worry, restlessness, fatigue Deep breathing, therapy, support groups
Depression Persistent sadness, loss of interest, low energy Counseling, exercise, talking to loved ones
Stress Headaches, irritability, sleep issues Time management, relaxation techniques, breaks
